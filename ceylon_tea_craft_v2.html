Our Heritage

Born in the
Misty Hills

"In the misty hills of Bulathsinhala, our story begins with carefully hand-plucked leaves from a single estate. Crafted using traditional methods, each leaf is shaped by hand into four unique forms — bringing out distinct flavours and aromas in every cup."

I

Tender two leaves & a bud

Only the finest young shoots are hand-selected at dawn.

II

Higher leaf quality

Meticulous grading ensures only the best reach your cup.

III

Hand rolling

Leaves are gently rolled by artisan hands to release their enzymes.

Natural · Organic · Whole Leaf

The Art of Single Origin Tea

Sustainably cultivated, mindfully processed — our whole leaf black tea is a direct reflection of the land it comes from.

✿

Plucking

Tender two leaves and a bud are hand-picked from our single estate at the optimal moment of flavour.

◈

Selection

Only carefully selected tender leaves pass through our artisans' hands — no shortcuts, no compromise.

◎

Hand Rolling

Leaves are rolled gently to release enzymes and lock in the distinctive character of each unique form.
